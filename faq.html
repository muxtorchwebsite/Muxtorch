Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

You can place an order by selecting a service on our website and providing the required details.

2. What are the delivery options?

We offer Express Delivery, Courier Delivery, and 2-Hour Delivery.

3. How can I track my delivery?

Once your order is placed, you will receive a tracking link via SMS or email.

4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money, credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery.

5. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via WhatsApp, email, or social media (@muxtorch).
